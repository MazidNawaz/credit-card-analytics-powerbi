## 📌 Dashboard Highlights

### 1️⃣ Quarterly Revenue & Transaction Volume
- **Q3** was the top-performing quarter with **14.2M revenue** and **166.6K transactions**
- Revenue remained stable across all four quarters (range: 13.3M – 14.2M)

### 2️⃣ Revenue by Age Group & Gender
- The **40–50 age group** is the highest revenue segment at **24.28M**
- **Female customers** contribute slightly more revenue than male customers across all age groups

### 3️⃣ Payment Method Breakdown
| Method | Revenue | Share |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Swipe | 35M | 63.6% |
| Chip | 17M | 30.9% |
| Online | 3M | 5.5% |

### 4️⃣ Top Expenditure Categories
| Category | Revenue |
|----------|---------|
| 🧾 Bills | 14M |
| 🎭 Entertainment | 10M |
| ⛽ Fuel | 9M |
| 🛒 Grocery | 9M |
| 🍔 Food | 8M |
| ✈️ Travel | 6M |

### 5️⃣ Revenue by Card Category
- **Blue card** dominates with **46M (84%)** of total revenue
- Premium cards (Gold + Platinum) together account for only **3M**

### 6️⃣ Customer Demographics
- **Businessmen** are the top revenue segment by job type at **17M**
- **Graduates** lead by education level at **22M**

### 7️⃣ Customer Satisfaction
- **Married customers** have the highest satisfaction score (**16.3K**) and are also top revenue contributors

### 8️⃣ Personal Loan Distribution
- **Female customers** (5.9K) have higher loan uptake than males (4.2K)
- **Graduates** are the most active loan takers by education (4.1K)

## 💡 Key Insights & Recommendations

1. **Core Revenue Segment** — The 40–50 age group and married customers are primary drivers. Targeted loyalty programs for this demographic would yield strong returns.
2. **Digital Payment Gap** — Online transactions are only 5.5% of revenue. Investing in digital adoption is a key growth lever.
3. **Card Portfolio Upselling** — Blue card dominates (84%), while Gold/Platinum are underutilized. Upselling to premium tiers can improve margins.
4. **Loan Opportunity** — Female customers and non-homeowners show higher loan uptake — a focused marketing opportunity.
5. **Satisfaction-Led Retention** — High satisfaction among married customers can be leveraged for referral and retention campaigns.
